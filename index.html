Frequently Asked Questions

We have tried to answer the most frequently asked questions here

What time should I reach?

Try to reach between 8:30 am and 09:30 am.

What if I reach late?

The registration counters closes at 09:30 am and everyone will be busy with the programmes afterwards. Try to coordinate with your SPOCs in case of delays

I haven't registered earlier. Can I still come for the event?

Yes you can come and do a spot registration.

Can I bring extra guests as spot registration?

No it is not possible. Guests should be registered in advance.

Will I get a refund if I cannot attend the event?

No. We have planned all arrangements based on the number of participants who signed up and amount collected accordingly.

Is there enough parking at the venue?

Parking is available. However, there are many new buildings now because of which parking is limited. We encourage carpooling to minimise the number of cars at the venue.

Can I leave the car at the venue while going for evening function?

No it is not advisable as there won't be any security. Try to find a private parking space outside the campus

What are the food and refreshment arrangements?

A buffet lunch and evening tea is arranged at the venue for all participants.

Will there be breakfast arrangements?

No breakfast will not be available

Which are the closest clinics/hospitals in case of emergency?

Nearest multi-speciality hospital is Daya General Hospital, State Highway 22, Viyyoor-Peringavu Rd, Peringavu, Thrissur, Kerala 680022 Phone: 0487 247 5100
